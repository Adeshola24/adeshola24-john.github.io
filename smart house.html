welcome to the smart house project

smart house

The smart house is a modified prototype of the NAEC house, i.e the way we intend the house to be like.

The smart house is truely a masterpiece because it's main purpose of creation is for security purpose,
most people nowadays are not safe especially in their home.

A home is the main place where a family resides and it is also the foundation of upbringing for all human beings in all aspects.

The smart house is made up of cartons,arduino cables,arduino board,sensors and so on.

The main advantage of this smart house is to reduce the rate of security breaching, in the sense
that in the smart house there is a sensor for fingerprint which opens up the house.

If there is a form of a theft,thieves won't be able to access the house without the owners fingerprint, which aids security

advantages of the smart house

  1. 1) It is comfortable and durable
  2. 2) It is artificially sensible
  3. 3) It is a way of bringing up young engineers and builders
  4. 4) It is a prototype made with arduino instuctions,e.t.c
  5. 5) It increases the rate of security
