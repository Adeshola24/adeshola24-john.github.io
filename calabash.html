what is a calabash?

A calabash is an artefact made from the hard shell of a fruit.

the calabash presented on this day is a very important gift because it is not only a gift but a decorated and well created calabash made with a lot of effort from a JSS2 student to the head of the NAEC committee

the calabash is used to store cool water for refreshing the body and mind

advantages of the calabash

  1. 1) the calabash can be used as a form of ornamental decorations
  2. 2) The calabash is used to store food and cold water
  3. 3) The calabash can be used as a gift or it can be used to present gifts to special guests of honour
