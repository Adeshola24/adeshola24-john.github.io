welcome to the smart dustbin project

smart dustbin

The smart dustbin is a modernized device, designed for disposing of refuse waste in a fun and enjoyable way



Why fun and enjoyable?

The smart dustbin is programmed with a set of instructions, for it to open whenever an object draws close to it.

This way am sure students will even like to dispose their waste properly to test such a wonderful invention.

Indeed it is a wonderful invention because it is made using cardboard,bucket, arduino and connectors

Advantages of the smart dustbin

  1. 1) It is quite fun for throwing dirts
  2. 2) It is artificially sensible and automatically opens when dirt is near
  3. 3) It is a good form for practising environmental sanitation
  4. 4) It is a prototype made with arduino instuctions,e.t.c
  5. 5) it is mainly made for people with disabilities to dispose their dirts easily
