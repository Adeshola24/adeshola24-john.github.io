welcome to the MRAP project

MRAP

The MRAP(Mine-Resistant anti-Ambush Protected)vehicle is an electrical Ambush-resistant and bulletproof vehicle which reduces the loss of manpower during war at the battleground.

The MRAP is a well developed vehicle in case of war but this project gives an insight in the development of the MRAP.

what are the changes?

1) The use of an automatic car with automatic turret to have a clear target without loss of manpower because it would be controlled from a distance

2) The MRAP should be able to carry a lot of passengers(soldiers) for war

3) It can be manually or automatically driven

advantages of the MRAP

  1. 1) The MRAP is an Ambush-resistant vehicle
  2. 2) The MRAP reduces the loss of manpower
  3. 3) The MRAP can move on bumpy or rough surfaces
  4. 4) The MRAP consist of an automatic turret which get it's target automatically without being manually used
