what is a periscope?

A periscope is an instrument containing lenses and mirrors used for observing over,around or through an obstacle or object which cannot be seen with our normal eyesight.

the periscope is indeed a work of art, the periscope can be used in submarines or used in the military defence force

advantages of the periscope

  1. 1) The periscope can be used by submarines for viewing obstacles ahead,around,above or beneath the surface of the sea
  2. 2) The periscope can be attached to tanks and armoured vehicles for war in other for them to view their surrounding at the battleground
  3. 3) The periscope is used in the laboratory to observe reactions occuring in the test tube or to measure te level of liquid in a container that is not easily accessible
